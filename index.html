The Summit

Leaders aren't born
at base camp.

FMCG: The Future of Leadership is SAAFoST's flagship gathering for the people moving fast-moving consumer goods forward.

Expect sharp keynotes, hands-on tools and the kind of room where the next decade of food science and FMCG leadership actually gets shaped. No fluff — just the strategies, technology and relationships that turn ambition into altitude.

Whether you lead a category, a plant, a lab or a start-up, you'll leave with a sharper map and a stronger rope team.

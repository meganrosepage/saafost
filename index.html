The Summit

Leaders are forged,
not born.

FMCG: The Future of Leadership is SAAFoST's gathering for the people moving our industry forward — and the leaders coming up behind them.

Whether you lead a category, manufacturing facility, research lab or a start-up, join fellow innovators to gain practical insights, forge valuable partnerships, and equip yourself to overcome obstacles and reach new summits of success.

This summit was built around three questions many FMCG leaders are already asking:

Now

What leadership skills do I need right now to lead well in this industry?

Future

What skills will I need as AI, digital tools and new generations change the way work gets done?

Next Gen

How do I spot, grow and keep the next generation of FMCG leaders before they walk out the door?
